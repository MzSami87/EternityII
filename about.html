About

Eternity Realty founded by Thomas Benjamin has been in business for five years. Thomas Benjamin born and raised in Mississippi. He is a graduate from Alcorn State University where he majored in Electrical Engineering. After graduating he landed a job at Microsoft in Atlanta, GA. There he was an engineer for 7 years. Finally he decided to pivot his career and realized real estate was the way to go. Thomas loves helping people and believes in generational wealth especially in the Black community. Eternity Realty is located in Las Vegas, NV. Thomas loves cultivating relationships, helping clients, and building communities. Eternity Realty not only specializes in residential homes but starting 2021 they will be acquiring commercial buildings as well. Eternity Realty is looking forward to the new direction the company is taking. Even though Eternity Realty has only been in business for a few short years they have won many real estate accolades: Top Producer of the Year, Good Neighbors Award, and Realtor of the Year.
